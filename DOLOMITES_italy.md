# DOLOMITES BIKEPACKING EXPEDITION — 18 DAY PLAN
## Italy — Alps / Dolomites Loop (WOW EFFECT ROUTE)

---

# OVERVIEW
This is a high-alpine bikepacking expedition through the **Dolomites mountain range in Northern Italy**.

You will experience:
- Vertical rock walls and sharp peaks
- Famous mountain passes (Tour de France / Giro d’Italia terrain)
- Alpine lakes and deep valleys
- Small Italian mountain villages
- Extremely high visual “wow density”

This is one of the most visually intense cycling routes in Europe.

---

# DIFFICULTY
- Physical: High (constant climbing)
- Technical: Medium (good roads, steep gradients)
- Weather: Variable (storms possible in mountains)
- Cost: Medium–High (Italy alpine region)

Daily distance: 50–90 km  
Elevation: very high daily climbing  
Style: road + light gravel + mountain passes  

---

# ROUTE STRUCTURE
Loop concept:
- Start: Bolzano / Trento region
- Core: Dolomites massif
- Key areas: Cortina d’Ampezzo, Sella Group, Val Gardena
- End: return south toward Trentino

---

---

# LOGISTICS

## FOOD
- Italian mountain huts (rifugi) available on routes
- Towns every 1–2 days
- Strong bakery + supermarket culture in valleys

---

## BIKE REQUIREMENTS
- Strong disc brakes (very important)
- Low gearing for steep climbs
- Tires: 32–45mm recommended
- Good heat + descent control needed

---

## WEATHER
- Summer: 10–28°C
- Storms possible in afternoons
- High altitude = sudden temperature drops

---

# COST ESTIMATE

- Food: €25–45/day
- Accommodation: €40–120/day
- Total trip: €900–2000 (depending on huts vs camping)

---
